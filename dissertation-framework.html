The mediation framework of Imai et al. (2011) structures this dissertation by guiding Paper 1's development of a valid mediator (CRI), informing Paper 2's analysis of mediator-outcome relationships, and directing Paper 3's estimation of treatment effects on the mediator, allowing separate analysis of TIA policy effects into direct and indirect pathways.
